Complete native rewrite of the Odysee app.

Added
- Native Android UI built with Jetpack Compose
- Faster startup, smoother scrolling, lower battery use
- Picture-in-Picture and background audio
- Cross-device watch position sync
- Server-backed playlists, watch later, favorites
- New wallet sync flow

FLOSS restrictions
- No Chromecast support
- No push notifications (periodic background fetch instead, defaults to 1 hour)
